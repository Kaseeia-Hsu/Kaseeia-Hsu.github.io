'Objects of Mundane' Workshop '22

In collab w/ Universidad Europea, Nagami, and C future Lab 深圳中洲未来实验室

Aug 17, 2022
1 minute read

Exploring Europe with my friends this Summer!

Here are some pictures taken from the workshop ‘Objects of the Mundane’ in collaboration with Universidad Europea and Nagami in Spain.

It’s such a great fun to see all the outcomes from these groups! Wishing that we all have the chance to see the final piece that were collected by museum in the future!

Above is a project looking at communication between machines, human and the ‘controlled errors’ occurred in miscommunication, Trying to create a catalogue that opens up a new way of design.

Above is a project exploring what ‘private’ spaces means, Deconstructing the ideas of privacy and intimacy of urban realm.
